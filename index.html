About Me

Hello! My name is Miao Jiang and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2015 when I decided that the company where I worked needed a website — Once I saw that shiny new website I built online I was hooked.

After that I went away and did a lot of online courses, coding bootcamp, and A LOT of tinkering on the web. I started my own freelance web design business specializing in building websites for coaches.

For over 10 years I've worked as the director in an educational company while running my freelance web design business on the side. While I've loved my years working as a manager in education, my passion really lies in tech and so I'm looking to transition into full working as a software engineer.
